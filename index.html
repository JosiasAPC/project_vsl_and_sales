About us

Bionorica SE - BioNature Inc.

History

Josef Popp, the grandfather of the current owner and CEO Professor Dr. Michael A. Popp, founded Bionorica on May 13, 1933, registering the “distribution of chemical-pharmaceutical preparations” at the Nuremberg Trade Supervisory Office.
He sold the first natural remedies and almost 400 different types of tea. The founder remained the head of the company for twelve years until 1945 when his daughter, Erna Popp, who studied pharmacy, took over the management of the company.
Together with her brother Dr. Hans-Oskar Popp, a doctor specializing in naturopathy, she laid the foundation stone for today’s company. By 1961, Bionorica already had a filling machine, a labelling machine and its first employees.
